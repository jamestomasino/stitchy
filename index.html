Stitchy is a crochet pattern generator. It's here to help you convert an image into a rectangular pattern of knots.

Choose your pattern size and the number of colors you'd like to use, then upload the image you want to use as a pattern. You can resize, move, and rotate the image. When it's just right, click “Render” and generate your pattern.

Once your pattern is rendered you can fix up any mistakes by clicking on the grid. The square you click on will cycle through the palette colors. When you are satisfied, click “Download” to save your work.
